Brown Butter Carrots

Notes

I picked up this recipe from a friend’s mother. She used brown sugar, but these days I replace it with molasses.

Makes

2 servings

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 6 carrots, sliced diagonally
  • 1 tsp molasses
  • Nutmeg, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste

Directions

  1. Cook butter over medium heat in a large saucepan until the milk solids are just starting to brown.
  2. Add all other ingredients, cover, and cook on medium heat until carrots are soft.
  3. Turn up the heat and cook uncovered until carrots caramelize around the edges.
