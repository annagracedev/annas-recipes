Coconut Orange Mango Juice

Notes

I don’t consume much raw fruit or vegetables. I’ve had varying reactions to them. Whenever I end up with fruit, I usually turn it into a juice and hope for the best. It’s almost too simple to be a recipe, but the proportions were really good this time, so I decided to take it down.

Makes

4 servings

Requires

  • Blender

Ingredients

Directions

  1. Press the oranges into juice with a citrus press. Do not blend the whole fruit. This is important to obtain the best flavor. I only blend oranges to make juice when I want to consume the pith for extra vitamin C.
  2. Add mango flesh, orange juice, and coconut water to a blender and blend on high speed.
  3. Strain the juice through a fine sieve. If you don’t mind the larger bits and pieces of mango pulp, you can skip this step.
  4. Serve immediately or refrigerate for up to 2 days.
