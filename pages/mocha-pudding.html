Mocha Pudding

Notes

I had a simpler version of cornstarch pudding often as a girl. This dessert is refreshing in the summer and easy to make even after a long day.

Makes

2 servings

Requires

  • Double boiler

Ingredients

  • 1 oz baking chocolate
  • 75ml heavy cream
  • 100ml strong coffee
  • 150ml milk
  • 3 Tbsp cornstarch

Directions

  1. Make coffee, add cold milk, and chill thoroughly. Whisk cornstarch into the coffee milk.
  2. Make chocolate ganache with chocolate and heavy cream.
  3. Remove ganache to a pot over medium heat. Add coffee milk mixture. Do not stir yet! Once the milk has heated up, begin whisking frequently and bring to a boil. When the mixture has thickened, remove from heat.
  4. Pour the mixture into moulds and set aside to cool. When cooled, place in the fridge to set completely.
