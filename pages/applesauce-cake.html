Applesauce Cake

Notes

I made this recipe because someone who can’t eat any fat was going to be at a party. I cannot tell a lie: the texture can be a little rubbery, especially if the oven temperature is miscalibrated. However, it was still quite edible.

Makes

8 servings

Requires

  • 8-inch springform pan
  • Parchment paper

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups gluten-free all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/8 tsp allspice
  • 1/8 tsp ginger
  • 1 apple, finely diced
  • 1 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1 Tbsp orange juice
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F.
  2. Line an 8-inch springform pan with a round of parchment paper.
  3. Combine dry ingredients with chopped apple. Add wet ingredients and stir thoroughly.
  4. Bake for 50-60 minutes.
  5. When the cake is done, a knife or toothpick may still come out with a few damp crumbs; it’ll set up more as it cools.
