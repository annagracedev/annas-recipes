Unorthodox Miso Soup

Notes

I’ve learned from my adventures with Japanese home cooking that there are countless miso soups. Here is my version of a very simple miso soup. Miso greatly improves the taste of chicken broth to me. Sometimes it is the only thing I want to eat.

The rice is fully cooked and cooled to increase levels of resistant starch, because I think it’s helpful for me. It’s also super convenient. Sushi rice may have more resistant starch than other varieties of rice. That’s how I justify my favorite sushi rice within my budget.

Ingredients

  • 250ml low sodium chicken broth
  • 1 Tbsp miso paste (I prefer white miso for this soup)
  • 1/2 cup cooked and fully cooled rice (optional)

Directions

  1. Combine broth and rice (if using) in a pot and bring to a low boil.
  2. Add miso and turn off the heat.
