Red Lentil Soup

Notes

This is the first recipe that I developed when I started cooking for myself. I’ve refined it since then, but the base of red lentil, tomato, and sweet potato has remained the same over the years.

Makes

4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 large sweet potato (or 2 medium sweet potatoes)
  • Olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium onion, finely diced
  • 1/2 package baby carrots, sliced
  • 1 cup red lentils, sorted and washed
  • 14.5 oz canned diced tomatoes
  • 32 oz low sodium chicken broth
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt to taste

Directions

  1. Boil sweet potato in water until the largest part tests done with a knife. Do this while preparing the rest of the soup.
  2. Cook garlic, onion and carrot in olive oil in a pot on medium-high heat until softened.
  3. Add lentils and spices; cook a few minutes longer. Add tomato paste and chicken broth; bring to a boil.
  4. Peel and dice the sweet potato. Add sweet potato to the soup and simmer until the lentils are cooked through.
