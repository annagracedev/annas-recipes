Coconut Hydrator

Notes

I tend to feel better when I hydrate with electrolytes instead of plain water. I try to cook with low levels of sodium so that I can control my sodium intake. Electrolyte packets are nice for their convenience. However, many electrolyte mixes contain high levels of vitamin B6, which can cause health issues when taken in high doses. Thus, when I run out of my favorite electrolyte mixes and have coconut water on hand, I usually prepare this mix instead of getting a different brand. Every type of salt has different sodium levels, so it’s important to check labels.

Makes

1 serving

Ingredients

  • 5 1/2 oz coconut water (about 300mg potassium, for me)
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt (about 900mg sodium, for me)
  • Splash boiling water
  • Water, to taste

Directions

  1. Dissolve the salt in boiling water.
  2. Add coconut water and dilute to preference. I normally end up with about 32oz.
